Why Parents Choose Sensory & Smiles

We're not just another toy shop — every product is chosen with purpose, tested for safety & designed for every child.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves access to joyful, purposeful play. Sensory & Smiles exists to bring together the best sensory toys on the market — rigorously vetted, thoughtfully curated, and affordable for every family.

🛡️

Rigorously Safety-Tested

All toys meet or exceed CPSC toy safety standards. Non-toxic, BPA-free, phthalate-free, and free from small choking hazards for young children. We go beyond minimum requirements because your child's safety is non-negotiable.

🧠

Therapist-Recommended

Our catalogue is reviewed by licensed occupational therapists who specialise in sensory integration therapy. Every item is chosen for genuine developmental benefit — not just marketing hype. Many of our toys are the exact same products used in OT clinics.

♿

Inclusive by Design

We serve every child — neurotypical, autistic, ADHD, sensory processing differences, and more. Our products are designed to be accessible across ability levels. We believe play is a universal right.

🌿

Eco-Friendly Packaging

All orders ship in recyclable, plastic-free packaging. We use soy-based inks, recycled cardboard, and compostable packing materials. Caring for kids means caring for their planet.

💰

100% Satisfaction Guarantee

30-day no-questions-asked return policy. If your child doesn't love it, we'll make it right with a full refund or exchange — every single time. Your happiness is our priority.

📦

Free Shipping Over £50

Orders over £50 ship free within the UK. Standard delivery in 3–5 business days. Expedited and gift shipping options also available at checkout.

How We Curate Our Toys

Every product goes through our rigorous 4-step vetting process before it reaches your home.

  1. Safety First — We verify all safety certifications and test materials for toxicity, durability, and age-appropriateness.
  2. Therapeutic Value — Licensed OTs review each product for genuine sensory development benefits.
  3. Real-World Testing — Products are tested by real kids and families before we stock them.
  4. Inclusive Review — We ensure every toy is accessible and beneficial across a range of abilities and ages.
