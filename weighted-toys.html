Why Weighted Toys Help Kids

Weighted sensory toys provide deep pressure stimulation (DPS), a therapeutic technique used by occupational therapists worldwide. The gentle, distributed weight mimics the feeling of a firm hug, helping to:

  • Reduce anxiety and stress
  • Improve focus and attention span
  • Support better sleep routines
  • Calm sensory-seeking behaviours
  • Provide proprioceptive input for body awareness

Deep pressure activates the parasympathetic nervous system, lowering cortisol levels and increasing serotonin and dopamine production. This is why many occupational therapists recommend weighted items as a first-line calming tool for children who experience sensory overload, meltdowns, or difficulty transitioning between activities.

Choosing the Right Weight

The general guideline is to select a weighted toy that is approximately 10% of your child's body weight. For a 30 kg child, a 3 lb (roughly 1.4 kg) lap pad is ideal. Our range spans 2 lb to 3 lb, covering children aged 3 to 12. All weights are evenly distributed using non-toxic glass microbeads inside removable, machine-washable covers.

When to Use Weighted Toys

Weighted plushies and lap pads are versatile tools that work across many daily situations:

  • Bedtime — Place on the chest or lap to encourage calm before sleep
  • Homework time — A lap pad keeps restless legs still and improves seated focus
  • Car journeys — Reduces anxiety during travel transitions
  • Classroom settings — Silent, discreet calming without disrupting peers
  • Therapy sessions — Used alongside OT-guided sensory diets
