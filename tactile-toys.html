The Importance of Tactile Play

Tactile play is crucial for child development. Exploring different textures helps children build neural pathways, improve fine motor skills, and develop tolerance for new sensory experiences. According to Pathways.org, sensory play supports:

  • Fine and gross motor development
  • Language and cognitive growth
  • Social interaction and emotional regulation
  • Sensory processing and integration
