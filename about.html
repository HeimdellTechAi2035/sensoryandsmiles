The Real Story Behind Sensory & Smiles

Sensory & Smiles was founded by Andrew James-Smith and Phillip Burton after Andrew's daughter was diagnosed with autism and he struggled to find affordable, high-quality sensory toys in the UK. Today, every product in the catalogue is reviewed by occupational therapists, meets EN 71 & UKCA safety standards, and is designed to help children with autism, ADHD, and sensory processing differences thrive through purposeful play.

How It All Started

Sensory & Smiles was born from a deeply personal place. Andrew's daughter was diagnosed with autism, and like so many parents, he quickly discovered just how important sensory toys are for development, regulation, and everyday comfort.

But there was a problem — quality sensory toys cost a fortune. The specialist shops charged premium prices. The high street didn't carry what was needed. And the cheap alternatives on marketplace sites were poorly made, sometimes even unsafe. Every parent in the autism and SEN community knows this frustration.

Andrew and his business partner Phillip said "enough is enough." If no one else was going to make quality sensory toys affordable and accessible, they'd do it themselves.

ðŸ§© "Every child deserves access to the tools that help them feel calm, focused, and understood — regardless of their family's budget."
— Andrew & Phillip, Co-Founders

That belief became Sensory & Smiles. Every toy in our collection is hand-picked with one question in mind: "Would we give this to our own children?" If the answer isn't an immediate yes, it doesn't make the cut.

We work directly with manufacturers, cut out the middlemen, and pass the savings on to you. We test every product for safety, consult with occupational therapists on developmental value, and listen constantly to the parents and carers in our community about what they actually need.

This isn't just a business — it's a mission. We're two dads who got tired of watching families struggle and decided to build something better.
